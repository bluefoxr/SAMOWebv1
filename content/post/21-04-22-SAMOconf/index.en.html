Abstract submission is now open for SAMO 2022! The conference will be held at Florida State University, Tallahassee, Florida. The dates of the conference are March 14 -16, 2022. The venue is Florida State Conference Center.

For more information, visit the conference website. We look forward to seeing you in Florida!
